Our Institute

Spreading the Word of Allah

Arresalah International Madrasah is a global online Qur'an learning institute dedicated to teaching students of all ages across Nigeria, Ghana, Sierra Leone, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

"Indeed, Allah will not change the condition of a people until they change what is in themselves." — Qur'an 13:11

Our mission is to spread the words of Allah with sincerity, excellence, and proper guidance.

  • Qur'an Memorization (Hifz)
  • Tajweed — proper recitation
  • Arabic Language (Arabiyah)
  • Islamic Studies — Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah, Nurul Bayan

Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Arresalah International Madrasah?

A global online Qur'an learning institute teaching students of all ages across Nigeria, Ghana, Sierra Leone, UK, USA, and beyond. We specialize in Hifz, Tajweed, Arabiyah, and Islamic Studies.

Can my five-year-old child apply?

Yes, we accept children from age 4-5. Our tutors use age-appropriate methods, and parents are welcome to join early sessions to ensure comfort.

Can I apply as a stay-at-home mom?

Absolutely! Our flexible scheduling accommodates family responsibilities at all levels.

Who can enroll in the courses?

Our courses are open to students of all ages (4+), backgrounds, and skill levels from all countries.

What is the difference between General and Personalized classes?

General Classes are group sessions offering affordable community learning. Personalized Classes provide one-to-one tutoring with customized pacing.

How do I enroll?

Click "Enroll Now" on your chosen class, fill out the form, and proceed to payment via Paystack or bank transfer.

How are classes conducted?

Online via Google Meet, Telegram, and WhatsApp. Sessions are interactive and recorded for review.

Is there a trial class available?

Yes! We offer a complimentary trial session before you commit to enrollment.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, upon successful completion students receive official certificates from Arresalah International Madrasah.

What if I miss a class?

All classes are recorded. Makeup sessions are also available for personalized classes.

What if I have technical issues?

Email arresalahinstitute@gmail.com or WhatsApp +234 808 206 2525.

What is included in Qur'an + Arabiyah classes?

Qur'an memorization, recitation, Arabic fundamentals, Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah, and Nurul Bayan.
