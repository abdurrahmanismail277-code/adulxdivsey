About Us

Arresalah International Madrasah is a global online Qur’ān learning institute dedicated to teaching students of all ages across different countries including Nigeria, Ghana, Sierra Leone, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

We specialize in:

  • Qur’ān Memorization (Hifz)
  • Tajweed (proper recitation)
  • Basic Islamic Studies

Our mission is to spread the words of Allah with sincerity, excellence, and proper guidance.
