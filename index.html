Our Institute

Spreading the Word of Allāh

Arresalah International Madrasah is a global online Qur'ān learning institute dedicated to teaching students of all ages across Nigeria, Ghana, Sierra Leone, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

"Indeed, Allāh will not change the condition of a people until they change what is in themselves." — Qur'an 13:11

Our mission is to spread the words of Allah with sincerity, excellence, and proper guidance.

  • Qur'ān Memorization (Hifz)
  • Tajweed — proper recitation
  • Arabic Language (Arabiyah)
  • Islamic Studies — Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah, Nūrul Bayān

Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Arresalah International Madrasah?

A global online Qur'ān learning institute teaching students of all ages across Nigeria, Ghana, Sierra Leone, UK, USA, and beyond. We specialize in Hifz, Tajweed, Arabiyah, and Islamic Studies.

Can my five-year-old child apply?

Yes, we accept children from age 4–5. Our tutors use age-appropriate methods, and parents are welcome to join early sessions to ensure comfort.

Can I apply as a stay-at-home mom?

Absolutely! We have many adult learners including moms. Our flexible scheduling accommodates family responsibilities at all levels.

Who can enroll in the courses?

Our courses are open to students of all ages (4+), backgrounds, and skill levels — children, teenagers, adults, elderly learners, beginners, and advanced students from all countries.

What is the difference between General and Personalized classes?

General Classes are group sessions — affordable community learning. Personalized Classes provide one-to-one tutoring with customized pacing, ideal for intensive or specific needs.

How do I enroll?

Click "Enroll Now" on your chosen class, fill out the application form, and proceed to payment via Paystack or bank transfer. Once confirmed, you'll receive login credentials and schedule information.

How are classes conducted?

Online via Google Meet, Telegram, and WhatsApp. Sessions are interactive, recorded for review, and scheduled at convenient times.

Is there a trial class available?

Yes! We offer a complimentary trial session for new students to experience our teaching methodology before committing.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, upon successful completion students receive official certificates from Arresalah International Madrasah recognizing their achievements.

What if I miss a class?

All classes are recorded. You can catch up on missed sessions at your convenience. Makeup sessions are also available for personalized classes.

What if I have technical issues?

Email arresalahinstitute@gmail.com or WhatsApp +234 808 206 2525. We provide full onboarding support.

What is included in Qur'ān + Arabiyah classes?

These cover Qur'ān memorization and recitation, plus Arabic fundamentals, Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah, and Nūrul Bayān.
